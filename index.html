Language Learner's Companion

Welcome to Language Learner's Companion, your personal GPT-powered guide to mastering English writing. This tool is designed specifically for non-native English speakers who want to improve their writing skills through practical and interactive learning. Whether you're writing an email, an essay, or preparing for an exam, this GPT is here to support you every step of the way.

### Key Features:

1. **Multilingual Support**: The GPT asks for your native language before you start, ensuring all instructions, explanations, and translations are provided in both English and your native language.

2. **Translation & Grammar Check**: It translates your text into fluent English, checks grammar, and provides detailed explanations of key points.

3. **Bilingual Responses**: The GPT answers your queries in both English and your native language, enhancing comprehension.

4. **Practice Exercises with Answers**: To reinforce learning, it generates exercises based on discussed topics, providing answers for self-assessment.

5. **Integrated Learning**: The GPT integrates English learning into real-world tasks, making language practice a natural part of your daily routine.
